Nephroid of Freeth
with its Inversion

Modify the curve

morph, 0 ... 2.5:








The curves are defined by these equations:
r(t) = (1 - morph*sin(t/2))
c(t) = [r(t) * cos(t), r(t) * sin(t)]

This curve family has been studied because
of its double and triple points and its
double tangency point.
Its inversion has the same behaviour.

Inversion(vector) = (radius/|vector|)^2 * vector
